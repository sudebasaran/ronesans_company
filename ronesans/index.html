Sustainability

Rönesans Holding is committed to balancing environmental, social, and economic impacts with its sustainability efforts. The company has implemented significant projects focused on energy efficiency, resource conservation, and the use of eco-friendly materials. Rönesans aims to reduce its carbon footprint in the construction sector, contributing to a more livable world for future generations. Additionally, the company supports sustainable development through its community responsibility projects and dedication to employee rights.
